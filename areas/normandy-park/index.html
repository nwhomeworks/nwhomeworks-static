Home Remodeling in Normandy Park

Kitchen, bathroom & whole-home renovation for Normandy Park, WA homeowners

Your Normandy Park Remodeling Partner

This neighborhood has a lot of homes with real character — mid-century bones, mature lots, and craftsmanship you don't find in new construction. Our job is to honor that and bring it forward. The kitchen you see here was functional, but it hadn't been updated in decades: tile counters, coil burners, dated cabinetry. We rebuilt it from the ground up, keeping the warmth of the original home while completely reimagining how it worked.

We handle every trade in-house — framing, electrical, plumbing, tile, cabinetry, and finish carpentry — which means one contractor, one point of contact, and no gaps between subs. That's how we stay on schedule and on budget, and it's why clients in this area keep referring us to their neighbors.

Kitchen Remodeling in Normandy Park

The kitchen is usually where we start. We've done full gut-and-rebuilds in the neighborhood — opening up closed-off layouts, relocating plumbing and electrical, and building out everything from scratch. Our most recent project here features dark walnut cabinetry paired with white quartz countertops, an arabesque tile backsplash, a Blue Star range with canopy hood, and a full wet bar with its own sink, wine fridge, and dedicated dishwasher. The clients wanted a kitchen built around entertaining. That's exactly what they got.

Not every project is a full rebuild — sometimes targeted updates to countertops, cabinetry, or appliances make the biggest difference. Either way, the level of care is the same. See our full kitchen remodeling services →

Bathroom Remodeling in Normandy Park

A well-done bathroom pays off every day — in comfort, in function, and eventually at resale. We handle everything from full master suite overhauls with custom tile showers, heated floors, and double vanities, to smaller hall bath and powder room updates where the budget has to work harder. Our tile team is meticulous about every detail, from large-format porcelain and natural stone to intricate mosaics, waterproof niches, and linear drains.

A lot of homes in this area have bathrooms that work fine but haven't been touched in 20 or 30 years. If you're looking for a trusted remodeler in Normandy Park — for a bathroom, a kitchen, or the whole house — this is the work we do. See our full bathroom remodeling services →

Basement Remodeling in Normandy Park

A lot of homes in this area have unfinished or underused basements, and converting that space is often the most cost-effective way to expand your living area — without touching the footprint of the house. We've built out full basement apartments, family rooms, home offices, guest suites, and rental units, all within the existing structure.

The basement kitchen pictured here came out of a complete lower-level overhaul — new electrical, plumbing, cabinetry, and finishes, fully permitted from start to finish. Most homeowners are surprised by how livable a well-done basement can be. If yours has been sitting unfinished, it’s worth a conversation. See our full range of services →
