Kitchen & Bath Remodeling in Bellevue

Home remodeling, kitchen renovation & bathroom remodeling for Bellevue, WA homeowners

Your Bellevue Remodeling Contractor

Bellevue homeowners expect quality — in the craftsmanship, in how the project is managed, and in the finished result. That’s exactly how we operate. With over 30 years in the trades, we handle every phase of your home remodeling project in-house: framing, electrical, plumbing, tile, cabinetry, and finish carpentry. One contractor, one crew, one point of contact.

This Bellevue kitchen started as a dated galley that felt cut off from the rest of the house. We took it down to the studs, opened the layout, and rebuilt it around a wraparound peninsula designed for cooking, eating, and gathering in one space. It’s the kind of transformation that changes how a family uses their home — and that’s where we do our best work.

Kitchen Remodeling in Bellevue

Every kitchen we build starts with the same question: how do you want this space to work? From there, we design and build around real life — how you cook, how you entertain, where the morning light hits. We’ve opened up walls, relocated plumbing and electrical, and rethought layouts that hadn’t been touched in decades. That combination of design thinking and trade skill is what makes a remodel feel like more than just new surfaces.

We work with all major cabinet lines as well as IKEA, and we’re just as comfortable building from the studs out as we are doing a targeted refresh that makes the most of what’s already there. Custom Built for How You Live

That same design-first approach carries into the details. We don’t just install cabinets and call it done — we listen to how you live and build solutions around it.

This Bellevue kitchen is a perfect example. The homeowners have a dog and wanted a way to keep food, bowls, and supplies out of the way without sacrificing floor space. So we designed and built a custom feeding station right into the base of the island — fully integrated, completely hidden, and purpose-built for their family. It’s a small thing, but it’s the kind of thoughtful problem-solving that turns a nice kitchen into the right kitchen. That’s what you get when your remodeling contractor is also your builder.

Bathroom Remodeling in Bellevue

Bathrooms demand the same level of care — and just as many trades working in sync. Plumbing, electrical, waterproofing, tile, cabinetry, glass — it all has to come together in a space where there’s no room for error. Because we keep every trade in-house, nothing falls through the cracks between subcontractors. The result is a bathroom that looks great and is built to hold up for years.

Whether it’s a full master suite overhaul or a compact hall bath where the budget has to work harder, we bring the same attention to detail.
