Custom Built for How You Live

One of the things that sets us apart as a Bellevue remodeling contractor is the custom work we build into every project. We don’t just install stock cabinets and call it done — we design and build solutions around how you actually use your kitchen.

This Bellevue kitchen is a perfect example. The owners have a dog they wanted to accommodate without cluttering the floor with bowls and bins. So we built a custom feeding station right into the base of the island — recessed bowls at floor level with dedicated storage above for food and supplies. It’s completely integrated into the cabinetry, clean, and out of the way. That’s the kind of detail you get when your remodeling contractor is also your builder.
