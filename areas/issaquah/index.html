Kitchen & Bath Remodeling in Issaquah

Kitchen renovation, bathroom remodeling & custom home improvement for Issaquah, WA homeowners

Your Issaquah Remodeling Contractor

Issaquah homeowners want a remodeling contractor who shows up, communicates clearly, and delivers work they can be proud of. That’s how we’ve built our reputation across the Eastside — one project at a time. With over 30 years in the trades, we handle every phase of your home remodeling project in-house: framing, electrical, plumbing, tile, cabinetry, and finish carpentry. One contractor, one crew, one point of contact.

For this Issaquah kitchen, we opened up the wall between the kitchen and dining room to bring in a ton of natural light and completely transform the feel of the space. Mixing materials — the warmth of butcherblock against the clean, crisp look of engineered quartz — creates a sense of balance that makes the kitchen feel both inviting and polished. It’s these kinds of thoughtful design decisions we bring to every project, and it’s why our clients keep coming back.

IKEA Kitchens with Custom Doors

One of our specialties is taking IKEA’s modular cabinet system and turning it into something that doesn’t look or feel like IKEA at all. This Issaquah kitchen is a great example — we installed IKEA cabinet boxes, then fitted them with custom fir doors and drawer fronts that bring warmth and character to the space. The result is a kitchen with the budget-friendly bones of IKEA and the look of fully custom cabinetry.

We’ve been doing IKEA kitchen installations across the Puget Sound for years, and we know how to get the most out of the system — from planning and assembly to custom modifications that make it your own. Learn more about our IKEA installation services →

Bathroom Remodeling in Issaquah

There are a lot of trades that need to come together for a bathroom — plumbing, drywall, tile, countertops, cabinetry, design — the list goes on. Fortunately, we have every trade that’s needed all in-house, and each are experts at their craft.

Take the tile installer who did this Issaquah bathroom. His meticulous attention to detail is on full display here — from the precision of the large-format layout to the way the linear mosaic accent ties the whole shower together. Custom Fireplaces & Built-Ins

We do a lot more than kitchens and bathrooms — like this Issaquah fireplace, for example. It’s another great example of our tile installer’s skill — the porcelain plank surround is clean, precise, and gives the whole wall a grounded, modern feel. The custom wraparound walnut shelves were designed and built in our shop specifically for this space, wrapping from the fireplace wall around the corner to create something that feels like it was always part of the home.

That’s what sets us apart as a remodeling contractor — we don’t just install what’s on the plan, we design and build solutions that fit. From tile surrounds and built-in cabinetry to floating shelves and media walls, we bring the ideas and the skills to make them real. See what else we can do →
