Kitchen & Bath Remodeling in Kirkland

IKEA kitchen installation, bathroom remodeling & custom home improvement for Kirkland, WA homeowners

Your Kirkland Remodeling Contractor

Kirkland homeowners want a remodeling contractor who can handle the whole project — not just the pretty finishes, but the framing, plumbing, electrical, tile, and cabinetry that make it all work. That’s us. With over 30 years in the trades, we keep every phase of your remodel in-house. One contractor, one crew, one point of contact.

This Kirkland kitchen is a great example of what happens when design thinking meets trade skill. The homeowners chose IKEA cabinetry to keep the budget in check, but you’d never know it — the mix of SOFIELUND wood-grain base cabinets and ABSTRAKT high-gloss white uppers gives the space a clean, modern feel that had their friends asking who built it. IKEA Kitchen Installation in Kirkland

We’ve been installing IKEA kitchens across the Puget Sound for years, and we know how to get the most out of the system. This Kirkland kitchen is one of our favorites — stacking the ABSTRAKT upper cabinets created a floor-to-ceiling pantry wall with a butcherblock baking station below, while the SOFIELUND lowers around the perimeter give the kitchen warmth and texture. Mixing two door styles is one of the smartest moves you can make with IKEA — it elevates the whole design without adding cost.

From planning and assembly to custom modifications and finish carpentry, we handle the entire IKEA installation so you don’t have to. Bathroom Remodeling in Kirkland

This Kirkland master bathroom had a layout that just didn’t work — the homeowners were climbing over the toilet to get into the shower, and a built-in bench was splashing water onto the floor. We expanded the bathroom by borrowing space from the adjacent bedroom, relocated the toilet for privacy, and rebuilt the shower with a frameless glass door and mosaic tile accents. The double vessel sinks and sleek dark vanity completed the transformation.

When every trade works under one roof — plumbing, electrical, waterproofing, tile, cabinetry, glass — nothing falls through the cracks. That’s the difference between a bathroom that looks good on day one and one that holds up for years. Read the full project story → See our bathroom remodeling services →
