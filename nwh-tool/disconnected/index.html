NWH Tool — Disconnected

NWH Tool has been disconnected from QuickBooks Online. The application no longer has access to read or write QuickBooks data on behalf of Pacific Homeworks Northwest LLC.

To reconnect

Authorized personnel can reconnect by launching the local NWH Tool application and clicking Connect to QuickBooks on the dashboard. The standard Intuit OAuth 2.0 authorization flow will restore access.
