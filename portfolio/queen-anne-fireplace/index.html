Queen Anne Fireplace Feature Wall

Seattle, WA

A dramatic living room transformation centered around a custom fireplace feature wall. We designed and built a floor-to-ceiling tile surround with an inset TV niche, flanked by custom mahogany storage cabinets with a Pacific Northwest touch — a live-edge madrone butcher block bar that doubles as a serving surface. The tile work extends across the entire wall, creating a stunning focal point that anchors the room.
