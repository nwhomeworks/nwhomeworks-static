Custom Doors, IKEA Cabinets

October 19, 2014 • By Tim Hutchinson • Apartments

Custom IKEA cabinet doors can completely transform a kitchen. This apartment kitchen features our own shop-made custom doors and walnut floating shelves on IKEA cabinet boxes — giving it a one-of-a-kind look at a fraction of full custom cost.

Custom Walnut Doors on IKEA Cabinets

For the accent doors, the customers chose flat-sawn walnut. One of the cool things about ordering custom IKEA cabinet doors is the grain matching, or sequencing, meaning the grain flows from one door to the next. In this kitchen, adding the warm, natural wood doors gives a perfect balance to the crisp, modern Abstrakt white.

This kitchen is a great example of how spending just a little extra on some accent pieces can turn a kitchen remodel into a real showpiece. The custom walnut doors and shelves, and the beautiful, hand glazed tile backsplash make this kitchen stand out without spending a ton of money. Another cool touch was the use of the white quartz on the island, and grey on the perimeter.

Before and After

I love before and after shots! Can you believe this is even the same apartment?! Seriously, what a transformation, and I’m sure nobody’s missing those pink (or is that mauve?) countertops right now!

Out with the old, and in with the new! In the old kitchen, Andrei (pictured above) would have been standing behind a wall, and in the middle of the peninsula. This new kitchen design has so much more room!

The Finishing Details

Because this is an apartment, there were restrictions on the type of flooring we could use. Hardwoods were out, and because of the open floor plan, the customer wanted one floor throughout the kitchen/living/dining rooms, so we decided on cork. Cork is a soft product so it’s comfortable under foot, but also fairly durable and should hold up very nicely for this small family. I know I already mentioned the backsplash before, but I really love that tile. I think too many people shy away from bold colors, but it seems like every time one of my customers takes the risk and goes for it, it pays off!
