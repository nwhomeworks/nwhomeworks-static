We're Back! NW Homeworks Blog Returns for 2026

February 21, 2026 • By Tim Hutchinson

Hey neighbors,

If you've just clicked on this blog, you might have noticed things went quiet for a while. Ten years quiet, actually! (Our last post was back in July 2016 — yikes!) But today we're excited to say — we're back, and better than ever.

First, a little honesty: keeping the blog (and the old website) updated while running a full-tilt remodeling business across Seattle, Tacoma, Kirkland, Bellevue, West Seattle, Bainbridge Island, and the rest of the Puget Sound just got… tough. When you're knee-deep in 100-year-old lath and plaster dust, surrounded by stacks of IKEA kitchen boxes, not to mention the endless paperwork and client emails, something has to give. The blog took the hit.

But here's the good news: those ten years of non-stop work have made us even better at what we do. We've picked up so many new tricks, smarter ways to stretch budgets, and real-world insights that only come from hundreds of finished projects. And we've missed sharing them with you.

That's why we decided it was time to resurrect the blog — and launch this shiny new website at the same time.

Why now?

We want to reach more homeowners who are dreaming about their next remodel. Whether you're in Tacoma looking for a practical laundry-to-mudroom upgrade, in Capitol Hill hoping to turn standard IKEA cabinets into something no one will believe is IKEA, or on the Eastside needing to overhaul a full bathroom that hasn't been touched since the 1950s — we want to be the resource that helps you get there.

We also just love sharing the knowledge we've gained. The little hacks that save thousands, the design decisions that make a space feel twice as big, the materials that actually hold up in our wet Pacific Northwest climate… all of it.

What you can expect going forward

Starting right now, we'll be posting regularly again in our own personal, honest, detailed style, with:

  • Lots of high-quality photos (the kind you can actually zoom in on)
  • Clear before-and-afters so you can see exactly what changed
  • Step-by-step project breakdowns
  • Inside tips and tricks we only share here
  • And plenty of real Puget Sound projects — no stock photos, no fluff

The first few posts you'll see coming up include: fully custom built-ins for a kids' bedroom (think creative storage that actually grows with them), a complete bathroom remodel in a home that hasn't been touched since the 1950s, another deep dive into IKEA PAX wardrobe systems and how we turn them into seamless custom closets, and a whole lot more — kitchens, living rooms, laundry/mudrooms, you name it.

We're also planning some fun "Ask Us Anything" style posts where we answer the questions we hear most from Seattle and Tacoma homeowners.

So whether you're just starting to research your project or you're ready to schedule a consultation, we're here and we can't wait to help.

Drop us a comment below if there's a specific type of remodel you'd love to see next (or if you recognize one of the upcoming projects — we might even feature your home!). And if you're ready to talk about your own dream kitchen, bath, or built-in project, reach out anytime — we answer every inquiry personally.

Thanks for stopping by our new blog. The best projects are still ahead.

— Tim & the NW Homeworks team
