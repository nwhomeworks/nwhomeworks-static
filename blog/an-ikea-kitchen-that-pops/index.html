An IKEA Kitchen That Pops!

September 9, 2013 • By Tim Hutchinson • IKEA Hacks

Here are some pictures from our latest kitchen remodel for some clients in Kirkland. Check out the bathroom remodel we did for the same clients here: Master Bath Remodel – Give Me Space!

IKEA doesn’t make a base corner cabinet with an angled front like this, but we were able to do a simple modification to their lazy susan cabinet which allows us to use this corner sink. The 17″ door from IKEA’s wall corner cabinet fits perfectly here. [Update 2026 – IKEA doesn’t make a 17″ corner door anymore with their new Sektion line, but this hack still works with an 18″ door]

This little wet bar area is oh-so-cool. The under cabinet LED lights really make things pop! The wine shelf is custom made using wall cover panels.
