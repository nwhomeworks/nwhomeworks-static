Beyond Kitchen & Bath

Every room deserves the same level of care

Same Team, Same Standards

Kitchens and bathrooms may be our specialty, but great craftsmanship doesn't stop at the kitchen door. Over the years, our clients have asked us to bring the same quality and attention to detail to every corner of their homes — and we've been happy to oblige. From dramatic fireplace transformations to smart storage solutions, if it involves skilled hands and creative problem-solving, we're in.

Living Spaces

A custom fireplace surround can completely redefine a living room. Built-in bookcases and media walls turn unused space into a design statement. Whether it's floor-to-ceiling shelving that finally gives your book collection a proper home, or a sleek entertainment center that hides every wire and cable, we design and build solutions that look like they've always been there — because the best built-ins feel like part of the house, not an addition to it.

Storage That Works

Closet systems that actually make your morning easier. Mudroom cubbies that keep the chaos contained. Laundry rooms that transform from an afterthought into a space you don't mind spending time in. These are the projects that don't get the glamour of a kitchen remodel, but they make an outsized difference in how your home functions day to day. We approach every one of them with the same planning, precision, and care that we bring to our largest projects.

Your Project, Our Expertise

What ties all of these projects together is the team behind them. The same skilled carpenters, tile installers, electricians, and plumbers who build our kitchens and bathrooms bring their expertise to every project we take on. That means your fireplace surround gets the same meticulous tile work as a master bath shower. Your built-in cabinets get the same precision as a custom kitchen. No matter the scale, the standard doesn't change.
