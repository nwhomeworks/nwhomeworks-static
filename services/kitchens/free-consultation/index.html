Common Questions

How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Seattle?

Kitchen remodels in Seattle typically range from $40,000 to $150,000+ depending on the scope. A cosmetic refresh with new countertops, backsplash, and paint starts lower, while a full gut remodel with structural changes runs higher. We provide detailed, transparent quotes so you know exactly what to expect.

How long will my kitchen remodel take?

Most kitchen remodels take 6 to 12 weeks depending on scope and complexity. A straightforward cabinet and countertop replacement is on the shorter end, while projects involving structural work, plumbing relocation, or electrical upgrades take longer. We give you a clear timeline before we start and keep you updated throughout.

Do I need permits for a kitchen remodel?

If your remodel involves structural, electrical, or plumbing work, yes. Cosmetic updates like painting or replacing countertops typically don't. We handle all the permitting for you, so you don't have to navigate the process yourself.

Can I stay in my home during the remodel?

Absolutely. Most of our clients stay home during their kitchen remodel. We set up dust barriers, use shoe covers, and clean up daily. We'll help you set up a temporary kitchen space so you can still function comfortably while we work.

What's included in the free consultation?

We visit your home, discuss your vision and goals, assess the existing space, and talk through options and budget ranges. There's no pressure and no obligation. It's simply a conversation to see if we're the right fit for your project.
