Bathroom Remodeling

Your personal retreat, built to last

The Vision

A bathroom remodel is one of the most personal projects in your home. It's the space where you start and end every day — and it should feel like it was designed just for you. Whether you're envisioning a spa-like master bath with a walk-in rain shower, updating a kids' bathroom that's seen better days, or adding a guest bath that impresses, we bring the craftsmanship and care to make it exceptional.

Tile That Sets the Tone

Tile defines a bathroom. It's the first thing you see and the surface you interact with most — and it demands precision. Our in-house tile team brings years of hands-on experience to every shower surround, floor, and accent wall. Whether it's large-format porcelain, intricate mosaic patterns, or hand-glazed artisan tile, they install it with the kind of care that turns a bathroom into a showpiece.

Built for Comfort

A great bathroom isn't just beautiful — it's comfortable. Our electricians install heated flooring that takes the edge off cold mornings, towel warmers that add a touch of luxury, and lighting systems that set the right mood from bright and energizing to soft and relaxing. Our plumbers engineer everything from multi-head shower systems to freestanding tub installations — because what's behind the walls matters just as much as what you see. Every connection is solid, every line is pressure-tested, and every fixture performs as beautifully as it looks.

Custom Details

It's the small, thoughtful touches that elevate a bathroom from nice to truly yours. A perfectly sized recessed shelf in the shower, built in our custom workshop to fit your space exactly. A floating vanity with soft-close drawers. Frameless glass that makes a small bathroom feel twice its size. We sweat these details because they're the difference between a bathroom that simply functions and one that becomes your personal retreat — a space so comfortable and beautiful that a long day melts away the moment you step inside.
