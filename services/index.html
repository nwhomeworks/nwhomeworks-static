Our Services

Expert remodeling for homes across the Puget Sound

Whether you're dreaming of a completely transformed kitchen, a spa-like bathroom retreat, or a smart IKEA solution that maximizes your budget — we have the skills, the experience, and the trusted team to make it happen. Every project is managed personally by Tim, from first consultation to final walkthrough.

Our kitchen remodeling covers everything from open-concept layouts and custom cabinetry to countertops, tile, plumbing, and electrical — whether you're working with a professional range or a tight apartment galley. For bathrooms, we build spa-like retreats with precision tile work, heated floors, frameless glass, and fixtures that last. Our IKEA installation service pairs their smart cabinet systems with custom doors, clever hacks, and professional execution — giving you the look of high-end custom cabinetry at a fraction of the price. And for everything else — fireplace surrounds, built-in media walls, closet systems, laundry rooms — our custom home projects bring the same level of craftsmanship to every room in your home.
